The Art of Satay

Southeastern Culinary Kabob Dish

What is Satay?
an Southeast Asian dish consisting of small pieces of meat grilled on a skewer and served with a spiced sauce that typically contains peanuts
Pork Skewers (Ingredients)
  1. 1 pound pork loin, cut into thin strips
  2. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  3. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  4. 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  5. 1 tablespoon lime juice
  6. 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  7. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  8. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  9. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  10. Salt and pepper to taste
  11. Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes
Peanut Sauce (Ingredients)
  1. 1 cup roasted unsalted peanuts
  2. 1/2 cup water
  3. 2 cloves garlic
  4. 1 shallot, chopped
  5. 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  6. 1 tablespoon kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  7. 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  8. 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  9. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  10. 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  11. 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  12. Salt and sugar to taste
Instructions to make Pork Satay
  1. In a bowl, combine the vegetable oil, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, ground coriander, ground cumin, turmeric, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the pork strips to the marinade, making sure each strip is well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat the grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the pork strips onto the soaked wooden skewers, making sure not to overcrowd them.
  5. Grill the skewers for 3-4 minutes on each side, until nicely charred and cooked through.
  6. Meanwhile, prepare the peanut sauce. In a food processor, pulse the roasted peanuts until finely ground.
  7. In a saucepan, heat the water over medium heat. Add the ground peanuts, garlic, shallot, tamarind paste, kecap manis, ground coriander, cumin, turmeric, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and sugar. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
  8. Serve the satay babi with the peanut sauce and any optional garnishes you like, such as sliced cucumber and red onion, fried shallots, and lime wedges. Enjoy!
